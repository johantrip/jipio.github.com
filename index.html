We are everywhere

Share what you own

With Jipio you can help your friends by sharing stuff you own. Easily add items and set a status.

Borrowed
Let’s say you’re going away for the weekend and need a tent, you can then go to Jipio and search for it. If you see a tent you like you can request the item and depending on the owner you can either borrow the item or even trade it.
Lent out
Jipio let’s you have a private conversation and schedule a pickup and dropoff time. The platform automatically reminds you to return an item. Items you’ve lend out are listed so you always know who has borrowed what and can ask to return it anytime. As an owner you can choose how to share the item and if you want something in return.

Vision

Together we waste too much. We waste money, resources and time by not putting to use what we already own.

Access thrumps ownership

We believe a new era is upon us with people sharing time, skills and things. An era where access trumps ownership and consuming directly from people we trust is more affordable, less wasteful and most importantly more social.

The Sharing Economy

The sharing economy is helping to pay the bills, work flexible hours, meet new people and spend more time with our families. It’s an economy that brings people together around a common good. We think it’s how the 21st century should work so we’re building the platform to make it happen.
